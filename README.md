# AOD Storyteller Archetypes - Peer Work Storytelling Discovery Tool

An interactive educational tool developed for NUAA (NSW Users and AIDS Association) that helps people discover their natural storytelling style in harm reduction and peer work contexts. Based on NUAA's SHARE & BUILD Peer Work Foundations Training.

## ğŸ¯ Purpose

This tool is designed for **the general public** - anyone interested in understanding how they might share stories about alcohol and other drugs (AOD) in meaningful, effective, and safe ways. Whether you have lived experience, work in the sector, or are simply curious about harm reduction storytelling, this quiz will help you:

- Discover your natural storytelling archetype (from 12 unique styles)
- Learn the core principles of effective peer work storytelling
- Understand boundaries and self-care in story sharing
- Connect with NUAA's deeper training opportunities

## âœ¨ Key Features

### Interactive Quiz

- 18 carefully crafted questions exploring storytelling approaches
- Sophisticated scoring algorithm identifying primary and secondary archetypes
- Progress tracking and intuitive user experience

### Comprehensive Results

- **Archetype Profile**: Your primary storytelling style plus secondary strengths
- **SHARE Principles**: How you embody Intentional, Purposeful, and Meaningful storytelling
- **Boundaries & Self-Care**: Personalized guidance using the Traffic Light System
- **Audience Awareness**: Tips for adapting your storytelling to different contexts
- **Universal Experiences**: How to connect through emotion rather than explicit details
- **Story Crafting**: Practical framework for developing your narratives
- **Reflective Practice**: Prompts for ongoing development
- **Resources**: Links to NUAA programs, downloadable templates, and support services

### Educational Integration

- Grounded in SHARE & BUILD training principles
- Harm reduction values throughout
- Connection to peer work expertise
- Gateway to professional development opportunities

10. **The Challenger** - Disrupting harmful assumptions
11. **The Navigator** - Guiding others through complex systems
12. **The Historian** - Connecting past to present, honoring context

## ğŸ“š About SHARE & BUILD

**SHARE & BUILD** is NUAA's AOD Peer Work Foundations Training program, designed to empower people with lived or living experience (LLE) in the AOD field to share their stories with confidence, safety, and impact.

The training explores:

- Crafting engaging personal narratives
- Developing empowering storytelling practices
- Adapting storytelling to diverse contexts
- Navigating boundaries and disclosure

This quiz tool introduces these concepts to the general public as a gateway to the full training experience.
